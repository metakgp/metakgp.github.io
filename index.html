Metakgp

What is Metakgp?

Metakgp is a loose association of engineers, hackers, artists, and students from IIT Kharagpur, who collaborate on various technical and non-technical projects.

We celebrate freedom and openness; all our projects are free as in speech (and usually beer too).

Some of our projects

Metakgp Wiki
The community wiki for IIT Kharagpur and our flagship project.
Question Paper Search
Instant search through previous years' question papers.
IIT-KGP Professor Search
Instant search for any faculty working in your area of interest.
KGP CDC Noticeboard
A Google Group where notices put up on the CDC noticeboard are mirrored, along with attachments. Subscribe for regular updates.
The Kharagpur Documentation Project
A collection of interviews of the non-student residents of IIT KGP.
Naarad
A news aggregator for collecting all news related to KGP.
